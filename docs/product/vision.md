# Product Vision

## Product Summary
RuleSet AI Marketplace is a marketplace product for discovering, buying, and selling AI-development assets such as rule sets, skills, MCP servers, workflows, prompts, agent configs, and bundles.

## Core Value Proposition
- Buyers can find production-ready AI workflow assets faster.
- Sellers can monetize specialized AI-development knowledge and automation packages.
- The platform can become a trusted distribution layer for developer-operational assets that are otherwise fragmented across GitHub repos, gists, docs, and chats.

## Product Surfaces
- Marketplace-led entry surface: discovery-first homepage, search, category exploration, featured inventory, trust signals
- Buyer product surface: marketplace, search, filters, product detail, cart, checkout, purchases
- Seller product surface: apply, dashboard, upload, analytics, payouts
- Admin product surface: moderation, user management, payout oversight, support operations

## Frontend Direction
- The primary first impression should be the marketplace itself, not a detached marketing page.
- The homepage should behave like a discovery-led marketplace landing with stronger product density, faster browsing, and clearer category entry points.
- Marketing should still exist, but it should support marketplace trust and conversion instead of dominating the entry experience.

## Strategic Risks
- Trust and quality are critical because digital assets can be low quality, stale, or misleading.
- Refunds, fraud, IP disputes, and support complexity will matter early.
- Search relevance and discovery quality will strongly affect perceived marketplace value.
- The platform should not over-promise production readiness before real verification systems exist.

## Product Positioning
The best positioning is not "generic AI asset store". It should emphasize curated, high-signal, production-minded assets for modern AI development workflows.
