# Admin and Operator Flows

## Seller Onboarding Flow
1. Seller applies
2. Admin reviews credibility and profile completeness
3. Seller is verified, rejected, or left pending
4. Verified seller gains upload and payout-related capabilities

## Product Moderation Flow
1. Seller submits or updates a product
2. Product enters review state
3. Admin checks quality, legality, category accuracy, and trust signals
4. Product is approved, rejected, or archived
5. Featured or sponsored placement is configured separately

## Support Flow
1. Buyer or seller raises issue
2. Support categorizes: payout, refund, access, moderation, trust, abuse
3. Issue is resolved directly or escalated to admin
4. Resolution is logged for auditability

## Trust and Safety Concerns
- fake reviews
- misleading screenshots or descriptions
- IP theft or copied assets
- payout abuse
- spam listings
- seller evasion after moderation actions
