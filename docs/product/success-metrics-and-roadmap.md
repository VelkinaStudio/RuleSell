# Success Metrics and Roadmap

## Success Metrics
- Buyer conversion from listing visit to purchase
- Search-to-purchase efficiency
- Refund and dispute rate
- Seller activation and retention
- Listing approval throughput and moderation quality
- Review quality and trust signals

## MVP
- Marketplace-first homepage and navigation
- Strong mock-data discovery, filtering, and product evaluation UX
- High-quality frontend with more product density and trust signals
- Seller onboarding basics in the UI layer
- Marketplace discovery basics
- Product-detail and browsing polish before backend integration

## V1
- Real auth
- Real checkout
- Real uploads
- Buyer dashboard
- Review system
- Admin moderation panel
- Email and notification flows
- Better search and ranking

## Later
- deeper seller analytics
- more trust and verification systems
- recommendation and personalization loops
- stronger SEO and content engine
