# Personas and Roles

## Buyer
- AI-native developer, solo founder, startup engineer, technical PM, or automation builder
- Wants trustworthy assets that save time and improve delivery quality
- Needs clear compatibility, proof of quality, and easy evaluation

## Seller
- Specialist creator with strong workflow, prompt, MCP, rules, or automation knowledge
- Wants distribution, revenue, discoverability, and credibility
- Needs uploads, pricing controls, analytics, verification, and payouts

## Admin
- Moderates submissions and sellers
- Reviews quality, policy compliance, trust signals, and disputes
- Oversees fraud, abuse, refunds, and platform configuration

## Support or Operations
- Handles seller onboarding issues, buyer disputes, payout issues, and content policy concerns
- Needs audit trails, status visibility, and escalation flows

## Key Role Relationships
- Buyers depend on seller quality and admin moderation for trust.
- Sellers depend on platform fairness, discoverability, and payout reliability.
- Admin and support functions are central, not optional, for a marketplace with digital goods.
