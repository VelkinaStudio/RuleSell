# Core Use Cases

## Buyer Use Cases
- Browse featured and filtered marketplace content
- Evaluate a product via description, compatibility, previews, tags, and social proof
- Add to cart and complete checkout
- Access purchased items later
- Leave reviews and manage refunds or disputes

## Seller Use Cases
- Apply to become a seller
- Create and upload listings
- Track downloads, revenue, reviews, and promotions
- Manage payout readiness and verification status

## Admin Use Cases
- Moderate products and sellers
- Approve, reject, archive, or feature listings
- Investigate disputes, fraud, and abuse
- Manage platform policy enforcement and trust signals

## Important Edge Cases
- Product becomes stale or incompatible after sale
- Seller submits misleading compatibility claims
- Buyer disputes download quality or value
- Refund requests conflict with digital delivery rules
- DMCA or policy complaints require takedown actions
